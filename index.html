About Us

Delivering excellence in electrical services for over 20 years.

Welcome to z.electric. Established in 2000, we are a family-run business spanning three generations. Our expertise includes security lighting, heating systems, fuse board repairs, and emergency electrical services. We are committed to providing reliable and efficient solutions tailored to your needs.

We proudly serve the greater Dublin area with a team of fully qualified electricians. All our work is certified and insured, ensuring the highest standards of safety and reliability. At z.electric, we believe in building long-term relationships with our clients through trust, professionalism, and exceptional service.
